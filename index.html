DIGIT: The Operating System for Public Service Delivery

DIGIT is an open‑source platform designed as shared infrastructure for governments to deliver secure, scalable, and cost‑effective public services. Its modular and interoperable architecture ensures seamless data exchange, enabling unified services, enhanced revenue, and coordinated governance.
